About Me

Hi I'm Jason. I'm a current PhD student in the Design Reasoning Lab at UCSC. My research is primarily focused on augmenting or utilizing search-based algorithms and machine learning for automated playtesting videogames. I create and test my testing frameworks and tools in Unreal Engine 5 using a combination of C++, Blueprints, and python code.
