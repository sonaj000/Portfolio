About Me

Hi, I'm Jason. I'm a graduate student in the Design Reasoning Lab at UCSC, where my research focuses on leveraging AI and machine learning for quality assurance testing in interactive media, particularly game engines such as Unreal 5. Beyond game development, I specialize in analyzing data from both algorithmic testing and player behavior analytics. My work involves developing AI-driven testing frameworks in Unreal Engine 5 using C++ and Python, while also applying data science and machine learning techniques to study player interactions, decision-making patterns, and in-game performance metrics. By combining game development expertise with data-driven insights, I aim to improve game testing efficiency, player experience, and AI-driven analytics in interactive environments.
